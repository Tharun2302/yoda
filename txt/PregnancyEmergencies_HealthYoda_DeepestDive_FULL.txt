SECTION 1 — EARLY PREGNANCY EMERGENCIES
=====================================================================

Ectopic pregnancy:
- Severe pain
- Bleeding
- Dizziness
- Shoulder pain
- Collapsing episodes

Threatened miscarriage:
- Bleeding + closed cervix

Q:
- “Bleeding or cramps?”
A:
- “Yes both.”

RED FLAGS:
- Severe unilateral pain
- Shoulder pain
- Syncope

SECTION 2 — BLEEDING IN PREGNANCY
=====================================================================

FIRST TRIMESTER:
- Implantation
- Miscarriage
- Ectopic

SECOND TRIMESTER:
- Placenta previa
- Placental abruption

THIRD TRIMESTER:
- Placental abruption
- Previa

RED FLAGS:
- Heavy bleeding
- Clots
- Abdominal pain

SECTION 16 — HEALTHYODA TRIAGE LOGIC
=====================================================================

ASK:
1. Gestational age?
2. Bleeding?
3. Pain?
4. Vomiting?
5. Fluid loss?
6. Headache?
7. Vision?
8. Swelling?
9. Fetal movement?
10. Chest symptoms?
11. Fever?

ROUTE:
- Pain + bleeding → miscarriage / ectopic / abruption
- Severe headache + vision → pre-eclampsia
- ↓ fetal movement → fetal distress
- Vomiting unable to drink → hyperemesis
- CP/SOB → PE
- Fever + tenderness → infection
