SECTION 2 — BACK PAIN
=========================================================

Q1. Where is the pain?
A: "Lower back." / "Neck." / "Mid-back."

Q2. Sudden or gradual?
A: "Sudden lifting box." / "Gradual."

Q3. Radiation?
A: "Down right leg." / "Into shoulder."

Q4. Numbness or tingling?
A: "Pins and needles." / "Numb toes."

Q5. Weakness?
A: "Leg feels weak." / "Difficulty lifting foot."

Q6. Bowel/bladder issues?
A: "Can't hold urine." / "Hard to urinate." / "No issues."

Q7. Trauma?
A: "Fell." / "Gym injury." / "No trauma."

Q8. Better or worse with movement?
A: "Walking helps." / "Sitting worsens." / "Bending painful."

BACK PAIN RED FLAGS:
- Saddle anesthesia
- Urinary retention/incontinence
- Progressive leg weakness
- Trauma + severe pain
- Fever + back pain
- Night pain + weight loss
