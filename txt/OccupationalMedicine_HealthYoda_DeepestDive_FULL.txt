=========================================================

- “Factory worker”

- Grinding/cutting
- Painting
- Welding
- Chemical handling
- Prolonged desk work
- Repetitive hand motions

DURATION:
- Acute (today)
- Subacute (days to weeks)
- Chronic (months/years)

- Mask?
- Gloves?
- Ventilation?
- Hearing protection?

- Anyone else sick?
- Similar symptoms among team?

SYMPTOMS:
- Cough, wheeze
- Rash

- Nausea/headache
- Pain (back, wrist, elbow)
- Tingling/numbness
- Hearing loss

A:
- “Chronic cough”

Fumes (welding, painting, industrial chemicals):
Q1. Chemical exposure?
A: “Welding fumes.” / “Paint thinner.”

Q2. Symptoms?
A:
- “Burning throat”
- “Coughing”
- “Headache”

ASBESTOS:
Q1. Renovation work?
A: “Removing old insulation.”

Chronic symptoms:
- Dry cough
- SOB
- Chest pain

RED FLAGS:
- SOB at rest
- Hemoptysis
- Progressive cough
- Weight loss

=========================================================

Carpal tunnel:
Q1. Repetitive typing/hand tools?
A: “Type 8 hours/day.”

Symptoms:
- Numbness in thumb/index
- Nighttime tingling
- Weak grip

Tendinopathy:
A:
- “Elbow pain from lifting”
- “Shoulder pain from repetitive overhead work”

RED FLAGS:
- Weakness + numbness
- Severe swelling
- Loss of function

=========================================================

Heat:

=========================================================
OCCUPATIONAL MEDICINE GLOBAL RED FLAGS
=========================================================
- Trouble breathing (any exposure)
- Chest pain
- Confusion, fainting
- Hemoptysis
- Severe abdominal pain (toxin concern)
- Neurologic changes (weakness, seizures)
- High fever with exposure
- Children exposed to workplace toxins carried home
- Radiation exposure with vomiting early
- Electrical injuries at work
