Q7. Fever?
A: "No fever." / "Low-grade fever."

PELVIC PAIN RED FLAGS:
- Sudden severe unilateral pain

- Pain + heavy bleeding
- Pain during pregnancy

SECTION 3 — VAGINAL DISCHARGE
=========================================================

Q1. Color?
A: "White thick." / "Yellow." / "Green." / "Gray."

Q2. Odor?
A: "Fishy smell." / "No odor."

Q3. Itching/burning?
A: "Severe itching." / "Burning with urination."

Q4. Pain with intercourse?
A: "Yes painful."

Q5. Fever?
A: "No fever." / "Low fever."

Common patterns:
- Cottage-cheese + itching → Yeast
- Thin gray + odor → BV
- Yellow/green + pelvic pain → STI / PID

DISCHARGE RED FLAGS:
- Fever + pelvic pain (PID)
- Severe abdominal pain
- Pregnancy + discharge + bleeding
