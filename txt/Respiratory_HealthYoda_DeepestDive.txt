===============================================================

Q1. When did the shortness of breath start?

Q2. Is it worse with activity, rest, or both?
- "Only when I walk."
- "Happens even when sitting."
- "Mostly at night."

Q3. How far can you walk before getting breathless?
- "Only a few steps."
- "One flight of stairs."
- "I can walk a block."

Q4. Can you lie flat comfortably? (Orthopnea)
- "No, I need 3 pillows."
- "I sleep sitting upright."
- "Yes, lying down is fine."

Q5. Any waking up gasping? (PND)
- "Happens nightly."
- "A few times a week."
- "No, sleep is fine."

Q6. Any chest pain with SOB?
- "Tightness when breathing deep."
- "Sharp right-sided pain."
- "No chest pain."

Q7. Any cough or sputum?
- "Clear mucus."
- "Yellow-green phlegm."
- "Pink frothy sputum."
- "Dry cough."

Q8. Any fever?
- "Had fever yesterday."
- "Low-grade fever."
- "No fever."

Q9. Any recent travel or long immobility?
- "9-hour flight."
- "10-hour car ride."
- "No travel."

RED FLAGS — Severe dyspnea, can't speak full sentences, blue lips, pink frothy sputum,
sudden SOB + chest pain, SOB + unilateral leg swelling.
