SECTION 2 — FEVER (GENERAL)
=========================================================

Q1. Highest temp?
A: "101°F." / "103°F." / "Low-grade."

Q2. Duration?
A: "Today only." / "3 days." / "Week-long."

Q3. Pattern?
A: "On and off." / "Constant."

Q4. Chills?
A: "Yes shaking chills."

Q5. Associated symptoms?
A: "Cough." / "Sore throat." / "Abdominal pain."

FEVER RED FLAGS:
- Fever + confusion
- Fever >103°F without cause
- Fever + rash
- Fever + low BP
- Fever + stiff neck
