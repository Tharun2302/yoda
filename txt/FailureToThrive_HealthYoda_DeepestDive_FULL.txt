SECTION 1 — GROWTH FAILURE DEFINITIONS
======================================================================

CLUES:
- Weight <5th percentile
- Weight dropping across percentiles
- Poor weight velocity
- Weight affected BEFORE height

Q1. Has weight changed percentiles?
A:
- “Dropped from 40th to 10th.”

RED FLAGS:
- Weight crossing 2 major percentiles  

======================================================================
SECTION 2 — NUTRITIONAL INTAKE ISSUES
======================================================================

BREAST/BOTTLE FEEDING:
- Poor latch
- Low milk transfer
- Tiring with feeds
- Infrequent feeds

SOLID FEEDING:
- Refusal of solids
- Low intake
- Grazing only

Q1. Feeding sessions duration?
A:
- “Takes forever and falls asleep.”

Q2. Feeding amounts?
A:
- “Not much.”

SECTION 3 — FEEDING TECHNIQUE PROBLEMS
======================================================================

CLUES:
- Incorrect bottle nipple flow
- Poor positioning
- Improper formula mixing
- Inconsistent schedule

Q:
- “Any difficulty feeding?”

A:
- “Formula seems too thin.”

RED FLAGS:
- Aspiration during feeding

SECTION 4 — ORGANIC CAUSES OF FTT
======================================================================

GI:
- Celiac
- GERD
- Food allergies
- Milk protein intolerance

ENDOCRINE:
- Hypothyroid
- Growth hormone deficiency

CARDIAC:
- Congenital heart disease
- Sweating with feeds

PULMONARY:
- Chronic lung disease

Q1. GI symptoms?
A:
- “Frequent spit up / vomiting.”

Q2. Sweating with feeds?
A:
- “Yes a lot.”

SECTION 5 — MALABSORPTION SYNDROMES
======================================================================

CLUES:
- Greasy stools
- Frequent stools
- Poor growth despite eating
- Bloating

Consider:
- Cystic fibrosis
- Chronic diarrhea
- Celiac disease

Q1. Stool description?
A:
- “Greasy / floating.”

======================================================================
SECTION 10 — GLOBAL FTT RED FLAGS
======================================================================
- Not gaining weight  
- Dropping >2 percentiles  
- Poor intake + fatigue  
- Sweating with feeds  
- Recurrent vomiting  
- Bloody diarrhea  
- Greasy stools  
- Developmental delay  
- Suspected abuse/neglect  
- Persistent dehydration  
- Decreased urine output  
- Persistent fever  

======================================================================
SECTION 11 — FTT TRIAGE LOGIC (HEALTHYODA)
======================================================================

ASK:
1. Feeding amounts?
2. Frequency?
3. Duration?
4. Vomiting?
5. Stools?
6. Wet diapers?
7. Sweating with feeds?
8. Development?
9. Illness?
10. Safety at home?

ROUTE:
- Poor intake → feeding technique
- Good intake + poor gain → malabsorption
- Sweating with feeds → cardiac
- Vomiting → GERD
- Greasy stools → CF
- Developmental delay → neuromuscular
- Unsafe environment → neglect
