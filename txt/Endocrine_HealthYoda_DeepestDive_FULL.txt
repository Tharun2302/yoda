SECTION 1 — UNIVERSAL ENDOCRINE HPI STRUCTURE
=========================================================

ONSET:
- "Started a few days ago."
- "Gradual over months."
- "Worsening slowly."

DURATION:
- Intermittent
- Persistent
- Fluctuating with stress

SEVERITY:
- "Mild but noticeable."
- "Moderate and affecting daily tasks."
- "Severe — interfering with routine."

PATTERN:
- Worse in mornings
- Worse after meals
- Worse in heat/cold
- Cyclic or triggered

ASSOCIATED SYMPTOMS:
- Fatigue
- Weight change
- Tremors
- Palpitations
- Heat/cold sensitivity
- Hair/skin changes
- GI changes (diarrhea/constipation)
- Mood changes

RISK FACTORS:
- Diabetes
- Thyroid disorders
- Family history
- Steroid use
- Amiodarone/lithium use
- Pregnancy

SECTION 2 — POLYURIA / POLYDIPSIA (THIRST + URINATION)
=========================================================

Q1. How often do you urinate?
A: "Every hour." / "All night long."

Q2. Amount?
A: "Large amounts." / "Little dribbles."

Q3. How thirsty do you feel?
A: "Constantly thirsty."

Q4. Any weight loss?
A: "Lost 10–15 pounds."

Q5. Blurry vision?
A: "Vision gets blurry sometimes."

Q6. Nausea/vomiting?
A: "Feeling nauseous."

POLYURIA RED FLAGS:
- Vomiting + severe thirst (possible DKA)
- Confusion
- Unable to drink fluids

=========================================================

Q1. How much lost?
A: "15 pounds in a month."

Q2. Intentional?
A: "Was not trying."

Q3. Eating more or less?
A: "Eating more than usual." / "Poor appetite."

Q4. Heat sensitivity?
A: "Always hot."

Q5. Urination changes?
A: "Peeing more often."

WEIGHT LOSS RED FLAGS:
- Weight loss + palpitations (hyperthyroid)
- Weight loss + polyuria (diabetes)
- Weight loss + neck mass

SECTION 5 — HEAT INTOLERANCE (HYPERTHYROIDISM)
=========================================================

Symptoms:
- Sweating
- Palpitations
- Tremors
- Anxiety
- Weight loss
- Diarrhea

Q1. Feel unusually hot?
A: "Yes always warm."

Q2. Tremors?
A: "Hands shaking."

Q3. Palpitations?
A: "Heart racing randomly."

Q4. Weight loss?
A: "Lost weight without trying."

RED FLAGS:
- Severe palpitations
- Eye bulging or pain

=========================================================
SECTION 6 — COLD INTOLERANCE (HYPOTHYROIDISM)
=========================================================

Symptoms:
- Fatigue
- Weight gain
- Dry skin
- Hair loss
- Constipation

Q1. Feel cold?
A: "Always cold even in warm rooms."

Q2. Hair or skin changes?
A: "Dry skin." / "Hair falling out."

Q3. Bowel changes?
A: "Very constipated."

RED FLAGS:
- Severe fatigue + confusion (myxedema)
- Very low heart rate

ENDOCRINE GLOBAL RED FLAGS
=========================================================
- Confusion + hypoglycemia/hyperglycemia
- Severe vomiting + fatigue (adrenal crisis)
- SOB + thyroid storm signs
- Severe dehydration
- Thyroid mass with airway symptoms
- Rapid weight loss + palpitations
- Very low HR + cold intolerance
