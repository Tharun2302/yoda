SECTION 1 — GLOBAL DEVELOPMENTAL MILESTONES
=====================================================================

GROSS MOTOR (examples):
- 4 mo: holds head up
- 6 mo: sits with support
- 9 mo: pulls to stand
- 12 mo: walks with help
- 15 mo: walks well
- 24 mo: runs

FINE MOTOR:
- Pincer grasp ~9 mo
- Scribbles ~18 mo
- Tower of blocks ~2 yrs

LANGUAGE:
- 12 mo: 1-3 words
- 18 mo: ~10 words
- 2 yrs: 2-word phrases
- 3 yrs: strangers understand speech

SOCIAL:
- Smiles by 2 mo
- Waves bye by 12 mo
- Parallel play by 2 yrs
- Cooperative play by 3+ yrs

SECTION 2 — MOTOR DELAY PATTERNS
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Late sitting
- Late crawling/walking
- Frequent falls
- Poor coordination

Q:
- “When did they start walking?”
A:
- “Still not walking at 18 months.”

RED FLAGS:
- Loss of motor milestones  
- Persistent toe-walking  
- Weak suck/poor head control  

=====================================================================
SECTION 3 — SPEECH & LANGUAGE DELAY
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Limited vocabulary
- No babbling by 9 months
- Not using 2-word phrases by 2 years
- Poor comprehension

Q1. Does child point?
A:
- “Not much.”

Q2. How many words?
A:
- “Only a few.”

RED FLAGS:
- No single words by 15 months  
- No 2-word phrases by 2 years  

=====================================================================
SECTION 4 — SOCIAL / EMOTIONAL DELAY
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Limited eye contact
- Difficulty engaging
- Prefers solitary play
- Trouble with emotions

Q1. Social interaction?
A:
- “Doesn’t respond to name often.”

=====================================================================
SECTION 5 — COGNITIVE / LEARNING ISSUES
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Difficulty learning letters/colors
- Poor memory
- Difficulty following multi-step directions

Q:
- “Can they follow simple instructions?”
A:
- “Not well.”

=====================================================================
SECTION 6 — AUTISM SPECTRUM PATTERNS
=====================================================================

BEHAVIORAL CLUES:
- Repetitive movements
- Spinning/lining up toys
- Fixation
- Rigid routines
- Sensory hypersensitivity

COMMUNICATION:
- Limited gestures
- No pointing
- Delayed speech
- Echolalia

SOCIAL:
- Reduced eye contact
- Does not play with peers

RED FLAGS:
- Regression of language/social skills  
- No pointing by age 1  

=====================================================================
SECTION 7 — NEUROMUSCULAR RED FLAGS
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Weak cry
- Feeding difficulty
- Head lag
- Tongue fasciculations
- Progressive weakness

Consider:
- SMA
- Muscular dystrophy
- CP

RED FLAGS:
- Loss of head control  
- Rapid motor regression  

=====================================================================
SECTION 8 — DEVELOPMENTAL REGRESSION
=====================================================================

MOST CRITICAL CATEGORY

CLUES:
- Loss of words
- Loss of motor abilities
- Loss of social skills

Q:
- “Did they used to do this and stopped?”

RED FLAGS:
- ANY developmental regression  
- Sudden or progressive loss  

=====================================================================
SECTION 9 — FEEDING/GROWTH ISSUES
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Feeding difficulty
- Choking
- Vomiting
- Poor weight
- Weak suck
- Fatigue with feeding

RED FLAGS:
- FTT (failure to thrive)  
- Aspiration risk signs  

=====================================================================
SECTION 10 — BEHAVIORAL DISTURBANCE
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Tantrums
- Aggression
- Hyperactivity
- Impulsivity

Consider:
- ADHD
- ASD
- Sensory disorders

=====================================================================
SECTION 11 — PREMATURITY RISKS
=====================================================================

CLUES:
- Low birth weight
- NICU stay
- Feeding challenges
- Development adjusted for gestational age

=====================================================================
SECTION 12 — GLOBAL PEDIATRIC DEVELOPMENT RED FLAGS
=====================================================================
- No smile by 2 months  
- No babbling by 9 months  
- No pointing by 12 months  
- No words by 15 months  
- No walking by 18 months  
- No 2-word phrases by 24 months  
- ANY regression  
- Persistent toe walking  
- Weak suck  
- Poor head control  
- Seizures  
- Poor weight gain

SECTION 13 — HEALTHYODA PEDIATRIC DEVELOPMENT TRIAGE LOGIC
=====================================================================

ASK:
1. Walking yet?
2. Talking yet?
3. Eye contact?
4. Gestures?
5. Regression?
6. Feeding issues?
7. Falls?
8. Behavior changes?
9. Premature?
10. Social engagement?

ROUTE:
- Regression → urgent concern  
- Delayed speech → language evaluation pathway  
- No walking → motor delay pathway  
- No eye contact → ASD  
- Weak suck/head lag → neuromuscular
