SECTION 2 — RASH (GENERAL)
=========================================================

Q1. When did the rash start?
A: "Started yesterday." / "Started 2 weeks ago."

Q2. Where did it start?
A: "On my arms." / "On my face."

Q3. Has it spread?
A: "Spreading rapidly." / "Staying in one spot."

Q4. Itchy or painful?
A:
- "Very itchy."
- "Painful burning."
- "Not itchy."

Q5. Any new products?
A:
- "New detergent."
- "New lotion."

Q6. Any new medications?
A:
- "Started antibiotics 5 days ago."

Q7. Fever or chills?
A:
- "No fever."
- "Had fever last night."

RASH RED FLAGS:
- Fever + spreading rash
- Mucosal involvement
- Painful red rash + rapid spread
- New medication + rash (SJS/TEN concern)
- Rash + trouble breathing (anaphylaxis)
